Frequently Asked Questions

Isn't registering clothes troublesome? +

It takes just 30 seconds - simply take a photo and select a category. Washing rules default to 3 wears and can be changed later. You can add detailed information like brand names later, so you can start simply.

Recording every time seems difficult +

Recording is completed with simple operation - just select item and date! If you forget to record, you can add past dates retroactively, so don't worry.

Do clothes really last longer? +

Yes. Washing is the most damaging activity for clothes. Avoiding unnecessary washing prevents fabric deterioration and significantly extends clothes' lifespan.

Is canceling Premium Plan easy? +

Yes, you can cancel anytime. For iOS, go to "Settings" → "Subscriptions", for Android, go to Google Play Store "Subscriptions" to cancel. You can continue using Premium features until the end of your billing period after cancellation.
