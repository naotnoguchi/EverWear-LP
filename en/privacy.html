Privacy Policy

Last updated: June 29, 2025

EverWear Office (the "Company") establishes the following Privacy Policy (this "Policy") to respect user privacy and appropriately handle personal information in providing the mobile application "EverWear" (the "App").

1. Information We Collect

The Company may collect the following information to the extent necessary for providing the App.

  • Information entered by users in registration forms, such as email addresses
  • Information about App usage (logs, device information, operation history, etc.)
  • Other information that users enter in input forms designated by the Company

2. Purpose of Use

The collected information will be used for the following purposes.

  1. To provide, maintain, and improve the App and related services
  2. For identity verification and user contact for troubleshooting and support
  3. To notify about new features, updates, campaigns, etc. of the App
  4. To provide important notices such as changes to Terms of Service and this Policy
  5. To create and analyze statistical data to help improve the App

3. Third-Party Disclosure

The Company will not provide personal information to third parties without user consent, except as permitted by law.

4. Information Management

The Company will implement appropriate security measures to prevent unauthorized access, loss, destruction, alteration, and leakage of personal information.

5. Use of Cookies, etc.

The Company currently does not use cookies, advertising identifiers, analytics tools, etc. However, if we introduce them in the future, we will update this Policy and appropriately disclose necessary information.

6. Requests for Disclosure, Correction, Suspension of Use, etc.

Users may request disclosure, correction, deletion, suspension of use, etc. of their personal information based on the Personal Information Protection Act. For details on procedures, please contact us at "8. Contact" below.

7. Revision of this Policy

The Company may revise this Policy in response to changes in laws or service content. The revised Policy will take effect from the time it is published on the App or in a manner deemed appropriate by the Company.
